Over two decades of Experience Experienced and Reliable Electrical Contractors

All Region Electric LLC's founder worked as an electrician in Hawaii and Seattle Washington for over 25 years before he decided to create an electrical contractor company offering high-quality, affordable electrical repair services. Because we recruit locally, our electricians are familiar with Washington electrical codes, so our service is always performed by someone who is both professional and approachable.

In 2022, we had just five electricians, but by 2023, we had expanded to over eighteen electricians serving the residential and commercial electrical needs of the entire Washington.

Certificates
We are licenced, Bonded, Insured & Certified Electrical Company

We now have a team of fully qualified journeyman electricians and a number of apprentices working with us. In the state of Washington, we are a licensed, bonded, and insured electrical contractor. Our goal is to keep our services high and our prices very competitive.
